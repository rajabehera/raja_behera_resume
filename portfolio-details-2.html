Sahyog App UI Design

The Sahyog App is a comprehensive healthcare platform designed to connect users with essential medical services. The app offers an intuitive interface for booking diagnostics, pathology tests, and doctor appointments. This solution aims to simplify the process of accessing healthcare services, making it more efficient and user-friendly.

Key Features

  • Login and Registration: A secure and straightforward login process that ensures user data privacy.
    • Phone Number Verification: Users can register and log in using their phone numbers, with an OTP verification process to ensure security.
    • Password Authentication: Secure password entry to protect user accounts.
  • Service Selection: Users can easily navigate through various healthcare services offered by the app.
    • Diagnostic Services: A wide range of diagnostic tests available from multiple labs.
    • Pathology Services: Comprehensive pathology testing options.
    • Doctor Appointments: Users can book appointments with doctors conveniently through the app.
  • Diagnostics Booking: The diagnostics section provides detailed information on available tests and pricing.
    • Lab Selection: Users can choose from multiple labs, with clear pricing information displayed for each.
    • Booking Confirmation: Easy booking process with instant confirmation and receipt generation.

Design Process

User Research and Personas: Conducted extensive user research to understand the healthcare needs of users. Developed personas to guide the design process.

Wireframes and Prototyping: Created wireframes to map out the app's structure and user flow. Developed high-fidelity prototypes to illustrate the app's functionality.

Usability Testing: Performed usability testing with potential users to gather feedback. Iteratively refined the design to improve user experience based on the feedback.

Visual Design

Focused on a clean and modern design aesthetic. Used a consistent color scheme and typography to enhance readability and ensure a cohesive look and feel. Emphasized accessibility and ease of use throughout the design.

Conclusion

This case study showcases the successful development of the Sahyog App, a user-centric healthcare platform. Through a comprehensive design process that included user research, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing, we were able to create an app that effectively addresses the healthcare needs of users.

Key Outcomes:

  • In-depth understanding of user requirements through extensive research
  • Iterative design enhancements based on user feedback
  • Visually appealing and functional interface
  • Streamlined access to essential healthcare services

The Sahyog App exemplifies the importance of user-centered design in developing digital solutions that meet and exceed user expectations. This project highlights the significance of a thorough design process in creating highly functional and visually compelling healthcare applications.
